About Me

Aladesae Adesina was born in Ibadan, on september 26, 1994. He lived in Ibadan for the most part of his childhood. He attended Staff School UI for his primary school, then proceed to Abadina College UI for his secondary school in 2004. During his post graduate studies he picked up an interest in cryptocurrency, web-development and blockchain development. Also, he loves Quantum Mechanics and general Astro-physics, he hopes to contribute to academics on Astrophysics in the nearest future. His brain child is crypto-shop, one of the leading web 3.0 platforms in Africa. He hopes to use the platform to develop Africa and provide employment. He is a big sport fan, he supports Liverpool FC and plays chess occasionally.
